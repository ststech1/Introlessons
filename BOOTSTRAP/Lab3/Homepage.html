Willdog's History

Willdog's pub started in the basement of a dog breeder's home in 1995.
People would come from all accross the state to enjoy the "One of a Kind"
Fried Pickles (The home of the fried pickle.). The dog-breeding business
was quickly left behind, and the home was renovated into a 5 star restaurant.
We now have 8 executive chefs working 24/7 who all live in the restaurant.
Ask your waiter about the bacon-ranch-hotsauce fried pickles today!
