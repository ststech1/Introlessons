Horizons

Parkway Drive

October 2007

Horizons is the second album by the Australian metalcore band Parkway Drive. Produced by Adam Dutkiewicz of Killswitch Engage, and released in October 2007. \m/\m/

Band Members

  • Ben "Gaz" Gordon - drums
  • Luke "Pig" Kilpatrick - rhythm guitar
  • Jeff Ling - lead guitar
  • Winston McCall - lead vocals
  • Jia O'Connor - bass guitar
